16th August 2023

Fenix Repower secures 140 MW order from Capital Power in Canada

In a significant stride towards bolstering Canada's renewable energy sector, Fenix Repower has triumphantly secured a monumental 140 MW order from Capital Power. This milestone achievement not only underscores the growing importance of renewable energy sources but also reaffirms Canada's commitment to sustainable development and a greener future.

A Game-Changing Partnership

Fenix Repower, a distinguished name in the global renewable energy arena, has yet again demonstrated its prowess by securing a colossal 140 MW order from Capital Power. This partnership represents a critical juncture for both companies and the Canadian renewable energy landscape as a whole.

Capital Power, a reputable player in the energy sector, has exhibited a keen understanding of the evolving energy paradigm and the pressing need to transition towards cleaner alternatives. By collaborating with Fenix Repower, they're not only diversifying their energy portfolio but also contributing significantly to Canada's ambitious renewable energy goals.

Catalyzing Canada's Renewable Energy Ambitions

The collaboration between Fenix Repower and Capital Power couldn't have come at a more opportune time. Canada has been making remarkable strides in renewable energy adoption and aims to generate 90% of its electricity from non-emitting sources by 2030. This order for 140 MW from Fenix Repower serves as a substantial leap towards realizing this objective.

The installed capacity of 140 MW holds the potential to power a substantial number of homes and businesses across the country. By relying on renewable energy sources such as wind or solar power, Canada can significantly reduce its carbon footprint and mitigate the adverse effects of climate change.

Technological Innovation and Sustainability

Fenix Repower's advanced technology and commitment to sustainability are two pivotal factors that have attracted the attention of Capital Power. As the world seeks innovative ways to transition from fossil fuels to cleaner alternatives, Fenix Repower's expertise in harnessing renewable energy resources positions them as a key player in this global transformation.

The 140 MW order is not just a mere acquisition of energy capacity; it's a testament to the engineering marvels achieved through renewable technology. Fenix Repower's cutting-edge solutions promise not only efficient power generation but also a more resilient and environmentally friendly energy infrastructure.
